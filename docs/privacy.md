# Privacy

Yohaku plugins are local Codex plugin and skill packages. They do not collect,
store, transmit, or sell personal data on their own.

The plugins shape prompts or specs inside the user's Codex session. Any data
handling by Codex itself is governed by the user's Codex and OpenAI product
settings and policies.

Yohaku plugins do not bundle apps, MCP servers, background services, analytics,
or external credential integrations unless a future plugin explicitly documents
that capability.
